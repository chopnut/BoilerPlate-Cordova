------------------------------------------
1. Add platforms after cloning the repo

 - cordova platform add android (--save to package.json)
 - cordova platform add ios@4.0 (--save to package.json) (supports ios8+)

 2. If you change something in the config.xml, you must do the following
    Add the platform again.

 - cordova plugin save
 - cordova platform rm [platform]
 - cordova platform add [platform]
